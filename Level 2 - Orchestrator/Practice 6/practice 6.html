Schedules

Scheduling a process is just like having Orchestrator press Start for you at a time which you specify.

Go to the Schedules page and create a new schedule. Identify your process (OrchestratorHelloWorld_DemoEnvironment) and try some of the options out. As shown in the video, you can instruct your process to start or stop either every couple of minutes, at a certain time, or on certain days.

The Execution Target section enables you to pick the Robots to run the process. All available robots, specific robots or a dynamically number of robots in that environment can execute it.

Notice the Stop Job after and Disable Schedule at from Actions tab. The Stop Job after property features the same two options - Stop & Kill.

Spend a couple of minutes playing with various schedules. Create some examples, play them one after the other, create new packages or processes, and pay attention to the order in which they are executed.
